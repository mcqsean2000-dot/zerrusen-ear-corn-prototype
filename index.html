Why whole ear corn

A familiar farm product for simple wildlife feeding.

Whole dried ear corn gives customers a recognizable, low-prep product they can place in feeders, storage bins, or seasonal stations. The store language stays practical: clean product photos, clear pack sizes, and honest notes about storage and feeding responsibility.

  • Dried for storage Set expectations around keeping bags covered, dry, and off the ground.
  • Whole cobs Easy for customers to understand compared with loose grain or blended feed.
  • Seasonal availability Inventory messages can change during harvest, winter, and spring demand.
