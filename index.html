Quality process

Cleaned, treated, packed to order, and boxed for shipping.

Whole dried ear corn gives customers a recognizable, low-prep product they can place in feeders, storage bins, or seasonal stations. Theo's Farm keeps the promise practical: high-quality cob corn, cleaned before packaging, treated to help protect the product, and shipped fresh.

  • Cleaned before packaging Husks and foreign material are removed, along with small or partially filled ears.
  • Freshly filled bags Every bag is packaged to order so customers are not receiving old inventory.
  • Protected for shipping Corn is treated with an Insect Growth Regulator before bulk storage and again when each bag is filled, then packed in a durable woven poly bag with a white paper overlay and boxed for added protection.
