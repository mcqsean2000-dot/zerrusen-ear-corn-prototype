The farm-to-feeder idea

Like farm to table, but for the backyard feeder crowd.

Whole dried ear corn gives customers a recognizable, low-prep product they can place in feeders, storage bins, or seasonal stations. Theo's Farm can own a simple promise: grown close to home, packed clearly, and ready for the feeder.

  • Dried for storage Set expectations around keeping bags covered, dry, and off the ground.
  • Whole cobs Easy for customers to understand compared with loose grain or blended feed.
  • Seasonal availability Inventory messages can change during harvest, winter, and spring demand.
